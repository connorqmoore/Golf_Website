About

Preferred Golf Systems is a club management solution that synthesizes cloud, mobile and tablet technology to effectively streamline your operation with real-time usable data. With over twenty five years of management experience at golf's most premier clubs, Preferred Golf Systems' team incorporates an intimate knowledge of the industry with the level of sophistication our clients demand, while also providing an easy-to-use and customizable platform.

Our goal is to maximize efficiency, increase productivity and elevate staff performance. At our core is the ideal that simplicity, through automation and technological innovation, incorporated appropriately with the game of golf, will allow employees to better service the club and its membership. Our goal is to assist clubs in adhering to the custom and tradition of golf's history while providing it with the tools to navigate the game's future.

Our products

Our products offer a user-friendly and intuitive cloud-based management systems for the club's membership and staff. Each solution is fully customizable to meet individual client needs for reservation, tee times, event registration and daily billing of usage fees to club members.

We offer reservation management systems that include: tournament and event registration, automated confirmation, online member access, seamless point-of-sale and billing automation, tennis court reservation management, and caddie and cart fleet management solutions, all through touchscreen user-friendly applications with tablets and mobile devices.

Our easy-to-use system is fully integrated with your current club website or homepage and we are accessible from anywhere there is Wi-Fi or network access.

Our products integrate seamlessly with most of the industry's established point-of-sale systems making billing fast and accurate.

Statistical reporting and data analysis of membership usage is customizable to any client's unique needs with easily exportable Excel-based reports.
